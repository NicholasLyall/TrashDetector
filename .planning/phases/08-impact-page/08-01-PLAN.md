<objective>
Build the Impact page with emotionally compelling environmental equivalents that make the system's CO2 savings and waste diversion tangible through real-world comparisons.

Output: A fully functional /impact page with a hero banner, CO2 equivalents grid (trees, miles driven), waste diversion stats (trash bags, elephant weight comparison), all wired to live useMetrics() data with loading/error states.
</objective>
